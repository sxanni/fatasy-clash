Creating a game that
-checks for a win, loose or draw

-Against a computer that randomly selects rock, paper or scissors

-if computer chooses rock
	and you choose scissors =(loose)
	and you choose paper =(win)
	and you choose rock = (draw)

-if computer chooses paper
	and you choose scissors =(win)
	and you choose paper =(draw)
	and you choose rock = (loose)

-if computer chooses scissors
	and you choose scissors =(draw)
	and you choose paper =(loose)
	and you choose rock = (win)
